A Toolset for Finding the Perfect Outfit

  • Face and Emotion Detection
  • Clothing Attribute Recognition
  • Related Clothing Retrieval

Project Goal

Imagine you have ordered an outfit online and are trying it on for the first time. You check yourself out in the mirror. You like the some things about outfit, but it doesn't fit you well, or maybe the style isn't exactly what you wanted. With our tools, a smart mirror would be able to take a snapshot of your clothes and show you similar items from across the web.

Or maybe you step out of a changing room at a department store to get an opinion on something from friends. A smart mirror equipped with our tools could be in the lobby and find you similar clothes from right within the store.

The clothes being retrieved are similar to the clothes being worn relative to the wearer's emotion when trying them on. A person that is pleased with the outfit they are wearing would be shown clothes with a higher degree of similarity, while those that are less enthused with their outfit would be shown clothes that are similar to the worn outfit but different in some aspects.

The benefit is ultimately for consumers and businesses. Consumers are assisted in discovering the outfits they want through a personally tailored experience. Businesses are provided valuable data on customers' reactions to their products, further traffic through product advertisement, and subsequently increased sales.
